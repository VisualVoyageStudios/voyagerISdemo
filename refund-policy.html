Refund Policy

Refund rules for Voyager digital tools and TradingView invite-only access.

Last updated: 2 July 2026 Voyager
Note: This is a practical template, not legal advice. Review it for your own business and jurisdiction before launch.

1. Digital product policy

Voyager products are digital TradingView invite-only tools. Because access can be granted immediately after payment, refunds are handled carefully to prevent abuse.

2. Before access is granted

If you request a refund before access has been granted, we will typically process it promptly — provided the payment has cleared and there is no suspected fraud.

3. After access is granted

Once TradingView access has been granted, refunds are handled case-by-case and are not guaranteed. We may consider:

  • Whether the wrong product was purchased by mistake
  • Whether access was granted and used
  • Whether there was a technical issue we could not resolve

4. Trading performance is not a refund basis

Market losses, dissatisfaction with trading outcomes, or unmet performance expectations are not grounds for a refund. No tool guarantees profits.

5. Subscriptions

Cancelling a subscription stops future renewals but does not automatically refund previous billing periods unless required by law.

6. Chargebacks

If a payment is reversed or disputed, access may be suspended or removed.

7. How to request

Contact us with your order email, product name, TradingView username, and reason: visualvoyagebsn@gmail.com
