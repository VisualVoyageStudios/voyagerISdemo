About

Built by a trader,
for traders.

Voyager is a small library of Pine Script tools built from real market experience — not theory, not repackaged open-source scripts.

What this is

Voyager sells invite-only Pine Script indicators and strategies for TradingView. Each tool is something I built and use on my own charts — tested across multiple markets and timeframes before it ends up here.

This is a software tools business. We sell analysis tools, not signals, not advice, not trade recommendations. What you do with a tool on your own chart is your decision.

What this is not

We don't provide trading signals. We don't tell you when to buy or sell. We don't manage capital. Nothing here constitutes financial, investment, or trading advice.
