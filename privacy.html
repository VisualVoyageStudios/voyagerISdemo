Privacy Policy

How Voyager collects, uses, and protects customer and order information.

Last updated: 2 July 2026 Voyager
Note: This is a practical template, not legal advice. Review it for your own business and jurisdiction before launch.

1. What we collect

We collect information needed to process orders and grant TradingView access:

  • Email address
  • TradingView username
  • Order and payment details from the checkout provider
  • Messages sent via email or WhatsApp

2. How we use it

  • To process purchases and verify payment
  • To grant and manage TradingView invite-only script access
  • To provide support
  • To prevent fraud, chargebacks, or unauthorised sharing
  • To comply with legal, tax, and payment provider requirements

3. Payment information

Card and payment details are processed by Lemon Squeezy. We do not store card numbers.

4. Third-party services

We may use third-party services for checkout, hosting, email, and messaging. Those providers operate under their own privacy policies.

5. Data retention

Order and access records are kept as long as needed to provide access, handle support, and meet legal and accounting requirements.

6. Your rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your information where legally applicable. Some records may need to be retained for legal or fraud-prevention reasons.

7. Contact

Privacy questions: visualvoyagebsn@gmail.com
